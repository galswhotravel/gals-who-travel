The Perfect 9-Day Thailand Trip for Solo Girls

✍️ gals who community📅 9 Apr 2026📍 Thailand

Thailand is magic! With turquoise waters that don't look real, floating bungalows surrounded by limestone mountains, island-hopping and elephant sanctuaries — it's the trip every gal should do.

Why Thailand is perfect for solo gals

Thailand has been topping travel lists for years, and for good reason. It offers an unbeatable combination of stunning natural beauty, rich culture, incredible food, and genuine warmth from locals.

Our 9-day Thailand Paradise trip takes you from the jungle-fringed floating bungalows of Khao Sok National Park all the way to the postcard-perfect islands of the Andaman Sea.

Day by day

Days 1–2: Khao Sok National Park

Arrive in Phuket, transfer to Khao Sok and settle into your floating bungalow on Cheow Lan Lake. The views will genuinely make you question whether it's real.

Days 3–4: Elephant Sanctuary

Spend a full day at an ethical elephant sanctuary — feeding, bathing and walking with these incredible animals. It's the highlight of the entire trip for most gals.

Days 5–9: Phi Phi Islands

Take the ferry to Phi Phi for island-hopping, snorkelling in crystal-clear water, cliff jumping and the most incredible sunsets you'll ever see.

I came back a completely different person. The friends I made, the experiences — nothing can top it. — Zoe, GWT Thailand Feb 2026
