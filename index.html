Questions

Things gals ask us

Is it safe for women travelling solo?
+
Thailand consistently ranks as one of the safest destinations for women. Our groups are women-only, guides are GWT-trained, and we have 24/7 emergency support. In 2025, every single gal reported feeling completely safe throughout.
Are flights included?
+
Flights to Phuket aren't included — book those separately via Skyscanner or Kayak. All internal transport within the trip is fully included.
Most gals come solo — will I feel like an outsider?
+
Not a chance. The vast majority join solo — it's the most popular way to travel with us. You'll be in the group chat before departure and will know everyone before you even get on the plane.
Can I pay in instalments?
+
Yes — £200 holds your place and you pay the rest at your own pace (60+ days before departure).
How big are the groups?
+
Groups are capped at 12 gals — small enough that you actually get to know everyone, not just the two people sitting next to you.
